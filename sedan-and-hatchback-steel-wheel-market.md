<p><p>The Sedan and Hatchback Steel Wheel Market is segmented into two primary types: casting and forging. Casting involves pouring molten steel into molds to create wheels, offering versatility in design and cost-effectiveness for mass production. In contrast, forging shapes steel by applying high pressure, resulting in stronger and more durable wheels, ideal for performance vehicles. Both methods cater to different consumer needs, with casting focusing on affordability and variety, while forging prioritizes strength and high-performance characteristics.</p></p>
