<p><p>The general influenza diagnostics market comprises various testing methods including Rapid Influenza Diagnostic Tests (RIDT), Viral Culture, Direct Fluorescent Antibody (DFA) assays, and Serological Assays. RIDTs provide quick results by detecting viral antigens, while Viral Culture involves growing the virus in a laboratory for identification. DFA uses fluorescent-labeled antibodies to visualize viral antigens in respiratory samples. Serological Assays measure antibodies in the blood to determine past infections. Each method offers unique advantages in terms of speed, sensitivity, and specificity.</p></p>
